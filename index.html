Fishie

Trade Smarter.
Play Fishier.

A variant of Jane Street's Figgie card game that adds deception and negotiation mechanics. Built on Figgie's core trading mechanics but with a twist - make deals, break deals, and try to coin flip your way to infinity.

Learn Figgie Rules

Game Variants

Slightly Fishie

Retractable Offers Cancel accepted trades before cards change hands
Selective Trading Choose who can accept your offers
Price Matching Post identical or lower prices for any suit

Very Fishie

Bulk Trading Offer multiple cards in package deals
Hand Revelation Pay to see others' current hands
Flexible Market Ends by consensus or 30-second timeout

Extremely Fishie

Rule Modifications Any changes with unanimous agreement
Pre-game Agreement Must be settled before round begins
Core Integrity Universal restrictions still apply

Universal Restrictions

No Cheating Unless unanimously agreed in Extremely Fishie
No Peeking At hidden information
No Theft Of coins or cards
No External Value Cannot trade using real-world items or currency
Important Once cards or coins physically change hands, all verbal terms of that deal become binding.
